Simple & Easy

Eureka emphasizes on simplicity, offering an easy project set up to get you started with your campaign whenever you're ready.

How it works

Eureka helps students raise funds on their creative projects or independant ideas to obtain the financial support needed by providing simple ways to create online fundraising campaogns and buid a large connected network of backers and supporters.

Industry partnership

Students are able to collaborate with external industries by marketing themselves and attracting new backers to their campaigns. Those industry partners could offer financial support as well as mentorship programs that equip the students with the knowledge needed to start a successful campaign or even take their projects to the next level.
